WHY BANKS REJECT

Why You Were Refused

Banks use automated systems with rigid cutoffs. If you fall outside their box — for any reason — you're rejected instantly, often with no explanation.

  • Credit score below their internal threshold
  • Missed payments or defaults on your file
  • Recent credit applications creating a footprint
  • Self-employed or variable income
  • Short time at current address or employer
  • No prior borrowing history (thin file)
