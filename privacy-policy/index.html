Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information

Legal & Transparency

This section explains how your data is handled when you visit our site or submit a financing inquiry

We connects applicants with a network of third-party lenders This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our website and any related forms or services By using our site, you agree to the terms described below

1

Information We Collect

When you use our site or submit an application, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Financial Details: Employment status, income level, and loan amount requested
  • Credit Profile: Self-reported credit range used to identify suitable lender matches
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and session timestamps collected automatically via cookies and analytics tools
2

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used exclusively to facilitate the lender-matching process and to improve our service Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Match you with relevant lenders in our participating network based on your financial profile
  • Communicate with you regarding your application status and financing options
  • Analyse site usage patterns to improve platform performance and user experience
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activity

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third-party marketers

3

Data Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Participating Lenders: Your application data is shared with lenders in our network for the sole purpose of evaluating your financing request
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who assist us with hosting, analytics, and communications, subject to strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or a valid legal process such as a court order or government investigation
4

Data Security

We take the protection of your data seriously All information submitted through our platform is transmitted using industry-standard 256-bit SSL encryption Our infrastructure is regularly audited and monitored for vulnerabilities

While we implement robust safeguards, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords and to contact us immediately if you suspect any unauthorised access to your account

5

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve site functionality, remember your preferences, and analyse traffic patterns Types of cookies we use include:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the site to function correctly These cannot be disabled
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our pages so we can improve the experience
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertising You may opt out at any time via your browser settings

You may disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings at any time, though some features of the site may not function as intended

6

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data under regulations such as GDPR and CCPA:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions
  • Right to Opt Out: Opt out of data sharing for marketing purposes at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@financedvehicles.com We will respond within 30 days

7

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Policy, or as required by law Application data is typically retained for up to 3 years from the date of submission You may request earlier deletion at any time by contacting our privacy team

8

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date shown on this page We encourage you to review this Policy periodically Continued use of our site following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy
