UNDERSTANDING NO CREDIT

No Credit History ≠ Bad Credit

Lenders who only read your credit report will turn you away because they see a blank file. Our lenders read your whole story — including what you earn and how you manage your money today.

First-Time Buyers

Never borrowed before? That's fine. We have lenders who specifically welcome applicants with no credit file and offer starter loan products.

Students & Graduates

Part-time income, student loans, or just starting your first real job — our lenders look at your potential, not just your credit file.

New Arrivals

Recently moved country? Your home country credit history doesn't transfer — but you can still qualify with proof of income and residency.

REQUIREMENTS

What You Need to Apply

Even with no credit history, you'll need to show these basics:

  • Age 18 or over
  • Proof of income — job, part-time, benefits
  • Active bank account (3 months minimum)
  • Valid government-issued photo ID
  • Proof of address (utility bill, lease)
CREDIT-BUILDING BENEFIT

Your First Loan = Your First Credit Score

Getting a car loan when you have no credit history is actually one of the smartest financial moves you can make. Every on-time payment is reported to credit bureaus and builds your score from scratch.

  • A loan now means a score in 3–6 months
  • A score opens doors to better rates later
  • Better rates save you thousands long-term
  • You get the car you need right now

Real result: Borrowers who start with no credit and make 12 on-time payments typically reach a "good" credit rating — unlocking lower interest rates on future borrowing.
