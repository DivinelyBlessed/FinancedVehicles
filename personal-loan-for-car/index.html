WHAT IS IT?

Personal Loan vs Car Finance — What's the Difference?

Both get you a car, but a personal loan gives you more control. Here's what makes it different — and why many buyers prefer it.

Cash in Hand

Funds are deposited directly to your bank account. You become a cash buyer — which puts you in a stronger position with any seller.

You Own the Car

Unlike HP or PCP, a personal loan means you own the vehicle outright from the moment you buy it — no balloon payments, no restrictions.

Buy From Anywhere

Dealers, private sellers, auction sites, Facebook Marketplace — a personal loan works with any seller, anywhere. No restrictions.
